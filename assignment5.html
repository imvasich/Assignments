Lovejoy: A band you can listen to whenever you want

Lovejoy is an Indie Rock band from Britain. The four members, Joe, Mark, Ash, and Wilbur,
came together to form the band about two years ago. THe four have put out two EPs since then,
which were produced by Anvil Cat Records. The EPs "Are You Alright?" and "PebbleBrain" have
been a huge hit, with their most popular song "One Day" having over 52 million streams on Spotify.
Very excited to see what they put out next!
